What are Logic Gates?

Real-World Analogy

Imagine you're managing a farm with chickens. Each morning you open the gate to let chickens out, and each evening you close it to keep them safe. This gate controls the flow of chickens and helps you achieve your goal of happy, healthy creatures.

Digital World

In computers, logic gates work similarly but control the flow of electrical current instead of chickens. They act as building blocks for digital circuits, performing basic logical functions that are fundamental to all digital electronics.

AND
Output TRUE only when all inputs are TRUE
OR
Output TRUE when any input is TRUE
NOT
Output opposite of the input
